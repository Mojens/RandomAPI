RandomAPI

Welcome to RandomAPI!

RandomAPI is a simple API that provides random animal and person data.

Endpoints

Endpoint Description Example Response

Usage

To use RandomAPI, simply make a GET request to one of the above endpoints.

The responses are in JSON format and include the following fields:

For /api/v1/animals:

  • species - The name of the animal species.
  • color - The color of the animal.
  • weight - The weight of the animal in pounds.
  • height - The height of the animal in inches.
  • age - The age of the animal in years.

For /api/v1/persons:

  • name - The name of the person.
  • age - The age of the person in years.
  • job - The person's job.
  • education - The person's education level.
  • address - The person's address.
  • phone - The person's phone number.
  • email - The person's email address.
